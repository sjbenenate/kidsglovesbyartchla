Our Story

Our founder saw that the kids in her grade school class were masters at mess making. This expertise caused much consternation with the parents when one teacher cannot take 15 minutes to wash every kids hands before school is over. This got her thinking on how to run her class smooth, keep parents happy, and still manage many fun (and messy) activites. Add the health concern of snack time and a remedied is mandatory.
Enter Artchla gloves, the peel and throw away clean-up.

Artchla Gloves make life easy.

Our Founder

Artchla Fay Patroni is a long time mother and leader in the community. Artchla Fay is a stalwart member of her church, and a loving wife. Her vision for the future of children's development is colored with art and education. Working as an art educator for over 23 years, she noticed that paints would stain children's hands during art activities. Parents would complain, and no one really knew what the health ramifications might be.

Her solution was fun and effective. Decorated vinyl gloves.
